Fried Rice - one of Cameroon's dishes

Delicious Fried Rice

Fried rice is a delicious dish loved by many and prepared by many in so many different ways. Fried rice is prepared in many different colors including brown fried rice, yellow fried rice, green fried rice and red fried rice. To make this fried rice attractive to the eyes and belly calling, some garnishing is add which maybe addition of canned corn, canned beans, carrot, green beans, chopped green spices.

Fried rice is on a high demand reasons mostly all big restaurants most have it on their menu and off course why not small restaurants and even some road side sellers who concentrate mostly at university junctions as most students will love to get a take away fried rice to ease the cooking fever from a long and tiring day at school.

People prepare fried rice differently depending on their budget and their likes too. But in all fried rice is such a delicious meal especially when prepared with chicken.

Ingredients

Below is a list of ingredients needed to make this delicacy

  • Rice
  • Chicken
  • Ginger and Garlic
  • Green spices
  • Carrots and Green beans
  • Onions and bell peppers
  • Curry powder and thyme
  • White pepper and hot pepper
  • Chicken spices
  • Groundnut oil
  • Sweet corn and peas
  • Salt and Maggie cubes

Preparation - steps

Steps to follow im order to make this delicious dish

  1. Step one
    • Wash the spices and blend together both the dry and wet spices
    • Arrange the chicken, spice it with some of the blended spices and boil
  2. Step two
    • Put a pot on the fire and add groundnut oil
    • Add the rice to the oil and fry using a wooden spoon until it becomes slightly brown
    • Add the chicken stock to the rice, add maggi and salt and some blended spices and stir well
    • Season the rice and allow to boil. add water gradually at this stage because the rice is not supposed to be sticky or too wet
  3. Step three
    • When the rice is almost ready and water have dried up
    • Cover the pot with aluminum foil pepper which helps to retain moisture and then cover and cook on low heat
    • When the rice is now ready, turn the rice on an open tray to cold down or open the pot and allow it to cool down
  4. Step four
    • Slice carrot and green beans and boil with a bit of limestone so that the colors don’t fade off
    • Slice onions and keep, wash the canned corn and beans and keep
    • Arrange all necessary spices and keep near so that you don’t forget any
  5. Step five
    • Put a frying pan on the fire, add a bit of oil and butter. Butter gives a rich creamy taste to the rice
    • Add the onions, green pepper, cut the chicken into smaller pieces and add to the frying pan fry for like two minutes.
    • Add the rice, curry powder, thyme, canned beans and corn and fry, make sure the mixture is stirred well
  6. Step six
    • Taste the check to check if Maggi and salt is ok, if not add a little.
    • And also if the rice is not well flavored, you can also add the blended spices and stir well.
    • The mixture should be homogenous.
    • The frying process can be done many times depending on the quantity and the size of the frying pan
  7. Step seven
    • When it’s ready, remove and put in a flask or a warm saving dish.
    • Now add the already boiled carrot and green beans to the rice.
    • It is added at the end to avoid it getting too cooked which can scatter in the rice and make it sticky
    • Food is ready. Enjoy your meal
